Italian

  1. Del Botri .
  2. Cap . CXXIIII .
  3. Il Botri è una herba folta , ramosa , rossa tutta , e sparta in molte ali .
  4. Il suo seme nasce attorno à tutti i fusti : le sue frondi sono simili alla cichorea .
  5. Respira tutta di soave odore : e imperò si mette ella tra i vestimenti .
  6. Ritrovasi nelle rive de i torrenti , e nelle valli .
  7. Bevta cura gli asmatici .
  8. Chiamano questa quei di Cappadocia ambrosia , e artemisia .

English

  1. Botrus is a yellowish herb like a shrub, broad- spread, having many wings, and the seed grows around all the branches.
  2. The many leaves are similar to chicory, and all of it has a wonderful sweet scent, and so it is also laid among cloths.
  3. It grows especially near running waters and brooks.
  4. A decoction ( with wine) is used as a drink to ease difficult breathing.
  5. The Cappadocians call this ambrosia, and it is also called artemisa.
